I am a Postdoctoral Research Associate at the University of Arizona working on problems in algebraic geometry. Previously, I was a Postdoctoral Fellow at the University of Toronto during the academic year 2022 - 2023. Prior to that, I was a Simons Postdoctoral Fellow at the Fields Institute for Research in Mathematical Sciences during the academic year 2021 - 2022. I received my Ph.D. degree in mathematics from the University of Kansas under the supervision of Purnaprajna Bangere. I obtained my B.Sc. and M.Sc. from Chennai Mathematical Institute, India. Here is my CV.

Here's a little bit more about me: I grew up in Kolkata, India. I spent five years in Chennai, India for my bachelor's and master's degrees before joining KU in 2015. Outside of mathematics, I am interested in literature, listening to music and watching movies.
