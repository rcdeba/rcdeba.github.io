I am a Postdoctoral Fellow at the University of Toronto at Toronto, Ontario, working on algebraic geometry under the supervison of Professor V. Kumar Murty. I was a Simons Postdoctoral Fellow at the Fields Institute for Research in Mathematical Sciences at Toronto, Ontario, during the academic year 2021 - 2022. I received my Ph.D. degree in mathematics from the University of Kansas at Lawrence, Kansas under the supervision of Professor Purnaprajna Bangere. I obtained my B.Sc. and M.Sc. from Chennai Mathematical Institute, India. Here is my CV.

Here's a little bit more about me: I grew up in Kolkata, India. I spent five years in Chennai, India for my bachelor's and master's degrees before joining KU in 2015. Outside of mathematics, I am interested in literature, listening to music and watching movies.
