My research interest is in algebraic geometry. More specifically, I am interested in the following areas.

  • Positivity of linear series and syzygies of projective varieties.
  • ACM and Ulrich vector bundles on projective varieties.
  • Topics related to Fourier-Mukai transforms and generic vanishing theory.
  • Deformation theory and geometry of moduli spaces.
